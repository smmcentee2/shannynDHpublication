About
Shannyn McEntee is a senior at the University of Nebraska – Lincoln. She is a double major in English and Classics and Religious Studies. She has an emphasis in Classics and a concentration in editing and publishing. She aspires to work on an acquisitions team for a publishing company. Shannyn grew up in Loveland, Colorado where she frequently visits to go hiking with her mini Australian Shepherd, Jemma. She one day hopes to hike every national park in the United States and has currently hiked 14/59.
